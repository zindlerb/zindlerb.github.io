Pork Chile Verde with Red Chile Salsa

Servings: 8 - Cook time: 30 min - Total time: 3.5 hours

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken Broth 3 cups (or more) divided
  • Tomatillos 1 pound divided
  • Green Onions 1 bunch
  • Fresh Cilantro with Tender Stems 1 and 1/2 cups (packed)
  • Garlic Cloves 6
  • Olive oil
  • Pork Shoulder (Boston butt) 4 to 4 1/2 pounds
  • Large Onion 1 (about 2 cups)
  • Cumin Seeds 1 tablespoon
  • Mild Green Chiles (such as Hatch or Anaheim Chiles) 3/4 cup
  • Dried Oregano (preferably Mexican) 2 teaspoons
  • Yukon Gold potatoes 1 pound
  • Cilantro
  • Red Chile Salsa

RECIPE PREPARATION

Prep Ingredients (10 min)

Chop Large Onion (1)

Trim pork shoulder (4-4.5 pounds)

Peel garlic cloves (6 cloves)

Coursely chop green onions (1 bunch)

Husk, rinse and cut tomatillos (1 pound) into 1/2-inch wedges

Dice, peel, and seed peppers (3/4 cup)

Peel and cut potatoes into 3/4-inch cubes (1 pound)

Make Salsa Verde (10 min)

Puree 2 cups chicken broth, 1/4 of tomatillos (1/4 pound), green onions (1 bunch), cilantro with stems, and garlic (6 cloves) in blender. Set salsa verde aside.

Cook Pork + Tomatillos (2.5 hrs) - Can be made 3 days ahead

Brush heavy large pot with oil; heat over medium-high heat. Sprinkle pork with salt and pepper. Working in batches, add pork to pot; cook until browned, turning occasionally, about 4 minutes per batch. Using slotted spoon, transfer pork to bowl. Pour off all but 1 tablespoon fat from pot (add oil if needed). Add onion (1) to pot; sauté until soft, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle cumin seeds (1 tablespoon) over; cook until onion is golden and cumin is toasted, about 2 minutes. Add remaining tomatillos; cook until tender and browned in spots, stirring occasionally and reducing heat to medium if browning too quickly, about 8 minutes. Return pork and any juices to pot. Add 2 cups reserved salsa verde, 1 cup broth, chiles (3/4 cup), and oregano 2 tsp. Cover; simmer over medium-low heat until pork is tender, about 2 hours. Chill uncovered until cold, then cover and keep chilled.

Cook Potatoes (30 min)

Bring to simmer before continuing. Add potatoes to pork. Simmer until potatoes are tender, about 30 minutes. Stir in remaining salsa verde; bring to simmer. Thin with additional broth, if desired. Season with salt and pepper.

Divide chile verde among bowls. Top with cilantro and dollop of Red Chile Salsa.

Divided - This ingredient will not be dumped into the recipe all at once. It will be “divided” or used in more than one place over the course of the recipe instructions.
